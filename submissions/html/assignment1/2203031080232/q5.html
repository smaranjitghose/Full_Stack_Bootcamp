Matar Paneer Recipe

Learn how to make Matar Paneer, a classic North Indian curry made with peas and paneer!

Ingredients

Gather these ingredients to prepare this delicious dish:

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen peas
  • 2 medium-sized onions, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps to Make Matar Paneer

Follow these simple steps to cook delicious Matar Paneer:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Mix in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Stir well.
  6. Mix in the peas and a small amount of water. Cover and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Add the paneer cubes and garam masala. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.

Tips

For the 2nd step, make sure to sauté the onions well for a richer flavor.
