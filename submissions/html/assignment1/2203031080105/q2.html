Steps to make Aloo mutter

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Base

Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds. Add the onion and saut√© until golden brown.

Step 2: Add Tomatoes and Spices

Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft. Stir in the turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.

Step 3: Cook Potatoes and Peas

Add the potato cubes and mix well. Add enough water to cover the potatoes and cook for 10-12 minutes or until they are tender. Stir in the green peas and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

Step 4: Final Touch

Sprinkle garam masala over the curry and mix well. for another 2 minutes . Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or bread.
