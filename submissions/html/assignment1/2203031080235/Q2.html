Delicious Chocolate Cake Recipe


Ingredients Needed

Here is the list of ingredients you will need to create this mouthwatering dessert:

    Dry Ingredients:
      2 cups all-purpose flour
      1 ¾ cups sugar
      ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
      2 tsp baking powder
      1 ½ tsp baking soda
    Wet Ingredients:
      2 large eggs
      1 cup whole milk
      ½ cup vegetable oil
      2 tsp vanilla extract
      1 cup boiling water

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these steps carefully to bake your perfect chocolate cake:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine all the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry mixture. Beat the mixture using a mixer for 2-3 minutes until smooth.
  4. Carefully pour the boiling water into the batter. Mix until it’s well combined. The batter will be thin, but don’t worry!
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove them and let them cool completely on a wire rack.
  7. Frost your cake with your favorite icing, and enjoy your 2nd-to-none chocolate cake!
