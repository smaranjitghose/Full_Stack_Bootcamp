Best Quotes According to Me

"Never forget what you are, the rest of the world will not. Wear it like armor and it can never be used to hurt you."

— Tyrion Lannister

This quote emphasizes embracing your true self with confidence, turning potential vulnerabilities into strengths that cannot be exploited by others..

"Power is always dangerous. It attracts the worst and corrupts the best."

— Ragnar Lothbrok

As the saying goes, the dual nature of power, suggesting it tempts those with ill intent and can morally compromise even those with noble intentions..

"Evil is evil. Lesser, greater, middling… Makes no difference. The degree is arbitrary. The definition’s blurred. If I’m to choose between one evil and another, I’d rather not choose at all."

— Geralt of Rivia

As the saying goes, the subjective nature of morality, rejecting the justification of any wrongdoing by degree and advocating for avoiding morally compromising choices altogether..

"No Tree can grow up to Heaven, Unless it’s Roots Reach down to the HELL."

— Unknown

As the saying goes, True growth and greatness often require enduring profound struggles and confronting one's deepest challenges..

"When you know how to win you may win many battles, but when you know how to NOT LOOSE you will win each."

— Jenish Paghadal

This quote underscores The importance of resilience and strategy, suggesting that avoiding defeat is a more consistent path to success than merely seeking victories..

"A Candle Loses Nothing When Used To Light Another"

— Stranger

This quote highlights The selfless and infinite nature of kindness, teaching that helping others does not diminish what we have but instead spreads light and positivity..
