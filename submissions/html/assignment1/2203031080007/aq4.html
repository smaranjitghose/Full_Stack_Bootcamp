Ordering & Delivery

Information about placing orders, delivery timelines, and charges.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order online through our website or contact us via phone for assistance.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Same-day delivery is available for select locations if the order is placed before 12 PM.

What are your delivery charges?

Delivery charges depend on the location:

  • Within 5 km: Free
  • 5-10 km: $5
  • Beyond 10 km: Contact for pricing
Payments & Refunds

Details about payment methods, cancellations, and refunds.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • Online wallets
  • Bank transfers
Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be modified or canceled up to 24 hours before the delivery time.

What is your refund policy?

If you're unsatisfied with your order, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery. Refunds will be processed on a case-by-case basis.
