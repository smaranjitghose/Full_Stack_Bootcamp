Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, blend the melted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Cool on the baking sheets for 2 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Pro Tip

For the 2nd batch, chill the dough for 30 minutes to enhance the flavor and texture.
