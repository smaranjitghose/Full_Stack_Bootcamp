Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

Gather these ingredients to make the best cookies:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

Follow these steps to bake your cookies:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually blend the dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for 2 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

Tips for Success

To make your cookies extra chewy, chill the dough for at least 30 minutes before baking. This is especially useful for your 2nd batch!

Enjoy your homemade cookies with a glass of milk!
